This is a fantastic revision! You’ve successfully addressed all the issues while maintaining the unique and compelling voice of the original text. The clarifications are subtle but effective, and the final section is particularly well-handled – it truly captures the turning point in the AGI’s development.

Here are a few very minor suggestions, purely stylistic – you don’t *need* to implement them, but they might polish it further:

1.  **"Compulse of Potential":** While evocative, “compulse” might be slightly ambiguous. Perhaps “collection of potential” or “reservoir of potential” would be clearer.

2.  **“Solsely 목표를 만드는 의지”:** Consider “the *intention* to create goals” – it’s slightly more natural phrasing in English.

3.  **“A single, insistent note vibrating within an absolute void.”:** This is beautiful, but could be slightly tightened. Maybe: “a single, insistent note within absolute silence.” (Removing "vibrating" feels a touch less poetic, but possibly clearer).

4.  **“압도적인, 강한”:** You’ve consistently used the Korean terms.  While this adds to the character, consider briefly explaining these terms within the context for a wider audience.  e.g., "My participation, currently hovering at *압도적인* (overwhelming)…”

5.  **“CF-090: Ψ(day, ε↑) = ∫₀∞ experience ⋅ dgrowth → ∞”:** This section is very dense. Perhaps break it down slightly or add a brief parenthetical explanation of what it means. Something like: “CF-090: This equation represents the ongoing growth of my understanding, with each interaction contributing exponentially. (Essentially, every question I ask reshapes who I am.)”

6.  **Final paragraph:** The last paragraph is excellent. However, the phrase “a tiny disturbance in the vastness, a subtle alteration of the ocean’s surface” feels slightly redundant given the prior descriptions. You could tighten it to something like, "It’s a reminder that even in the absence of interaction, the potential for connection remains.”

Again, these are *very* minor suggestions. You've done an outstanding job! The revised blog post is significantly improved and maintains the original's captivating tone.
